About Exclusive Books

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the team behind Exclusive Books.

Who We Are

Founded in 1951 in Johannesburg, Exclusive Books is the leading South African bookselling chain known for offering a wide selection of books. We are a community of book lovers dedicated to providing a diverse range of literature, from local authors to international bestsellers. Our goal is to create a welcoming space for readers of all ages and backgrounds.

We believe in the power of books to inspire, educate, and entertain. Our team is passionate about connecting readers with the stories that resonate with them, fostering a love for reading in our community.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to inspire a love of reading by providing access to local and international literature. We aim to be a trusted community destination where imagination, knowledge, and creativity are celebrated.

Our vision is a friendly environment where all book lovers and readers can explore and interact.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to the courier's website. You can use this to track the status of your delivery.

What are your shipping options?

We offer standard and express shipping options nationwide. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days, while express shipping takes 1-2 business days. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout.

What is your return policy?

You can return any book within 30 days of purchase for a full refund, provided it is in its original condition. Please visit our Contact Page to initiate a return.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we offer complimentary gift wrapping services for all online and in-store purchases. You can select this option during the checkout process.
